Hamare baad mehfil mein afsaane bayan honge, Bahaaren humko dhoondhegi, na jaane hum kahan honge. Kisi ki aankh royegi, kisi ke hoth hans denge, Kisi ke dil mein reh-reh kar humare daag jaga honge. Humein to waqt ki gardish nigal gayi khaamoshi se, Magar kahani kehne waale sadiyon talak zinda honge.
